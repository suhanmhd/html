About Us

Founded in 1999 with a passionate conviction for uncompromising quality and a never ending desire for fulfilling customer needs, Nikunjam registered a steady growth in the Real Estate Sector. Our success has been rooted in the strong foundation of innovation, teamwork and technical expertise combined with a high degree of business commitment & ethics. Today, Nikunjam is reckoned as a synonym for quality construction both in residential projects and commercial spaces and have delivered all our projects within the committed timelines. This commitment has led to the high level of confidence and goodwill of thousands of satisfied customers in the Nikunjam family.At Nikunjam we believe that our home owners are our most valuable asset. The long term relationship with them and repeat orders and referral sales we get from them stand testimony to our business philosophy. Nikunjam enjoys an enviable track of handing over projects on time which we believe is the key to customer satisfaction. Continual updating of technical expertise, and adhering to high safety and other industry standards helped us to keep the deadlines sharp and deliver the projects on time.
